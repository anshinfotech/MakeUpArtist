About Us

Welcome bring the best service!

Welcome to Palka Arora Makeup Artist, where beauty meets artistry. With over 9+ years of experience in the makeup industry, Palka Arora has established herself as a trusted name in Ludhiana, Punjab. Specializing in bridal makeup, editorial looks, and personalized beauty solutions, Palka’s expertise lies in enhancing natural beauty while reflecting each client's unique style and personality.

Whether it’s for your big day, a special event, or a professional photoshoot, Palka ensures that you leave with a flawless, long-lasting look that makes you feel confident and radiant. Her commitment to using only the highest quality products, combined with her passion for the art of makeup, guarantees a luxurious and personalized experience for every client.

At Palka Arora Makeup Artist, we believe that beauty is not just about appearance, but also about confidence and self-expression. Let us help you celebrate your beauty with our expert services tailored just for you.
